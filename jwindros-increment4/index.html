April’s Feature: Wayne McGregor’s Infinite Bodies

Welcome to the MonoMuse Museum

Visit MonoMuse Museum this month for a chance to experience Wayne McGregor’s Infinite Bodies! The exhibitions embraces dance and technology by uncovering how the human body can incorporate data and AI to act as both an instrument and a performer. The multi-sensory, interactive exhibition will be housed in MonoMuse Museum for the month of April. Wayne McGregor, the artist behind Infinite Bodies, has collaborated with other artists, designers, filmmakers, and musicians to bring this immersive event to life. This amazing exhibition is worthwhile for people of all ages. Visit MonoMuse throughout the week, Monday - Sunday from 9AM to 9PM, to experience Wayne McGregor’s Infinite Bodies. Relaxed sessions will also be held for those with neurodiverse needs, as our purpose ar MonoMuse Museum is to cater to all of our guests' needs because everyone deserves to experience the museum’s exceptional offerings. We hope to see you soon! To purchase tickets, please visit the Buy Tickets page and for quick-access select the Buy Tickets button below.
