Exhibition 1: Textile & Abstraction

This exhibition explores how artists use textile to express identity, labor, and culture. Through the process of knitting, felting, embroidery, and/or weaving, artists are able to tell a story with geometric patterns. The exhibition highlight fiber-based crafts with a theme of modernism and resistance. International artists join together to share their craft and story.

Youth & Imagination: The Craft of Young Innovators

Youth & Imagination Night highlights the hard-work and dedication of students participating in the Imagination Station Scholars program. As the school-year comes to an end, students display their long-term projects, focusing on the intersection of art, technology, and society.
