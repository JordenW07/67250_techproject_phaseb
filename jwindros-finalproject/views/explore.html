Mission Statement

MonoMuse Museum seeks to encourage the exploration of art, technology, and society. Our mission is to connect curious minds with innovative exhibitions and workshops. By incorporating contemporary technology and ideas, we hope to establish and maintain creative production and innovative thinking.

Milestones

  • 2025: MonoMuse builds two new wings dedicated to AI and architecture.
  • 2010: MonoMuse partners with the local school district to launch its Imagination Station. Students learn various art styles, use innovative technology, and learn about historical movements during workshops hosted in the Imagination Station.
  • 2007: MonoMuse makes an introduction by hosting its first exhibition on textile and modern abstraction.
